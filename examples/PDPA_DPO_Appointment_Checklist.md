# PDPA Data Protection Officer (DPO) Appointment Checklist

**For Route A v1.1.0 - PDPA Profile (dpo_required=True)**

## Appointment Requirements
- [ ] Designate at least one DPO (can be internal or external)
- [ ] Ensure DPO has relevant expertise in data protection (PDPA, cross-border)
- [ ] Document DPO contact details (name, email, phone) in compliance records
- [ ] Notify PDPC of DPO appointment if required for organization size

## DPO Responsibilities
- [ ] Oversee compliance with PDPA consent, access, correction obligations
- [ ] Handle 30-day access/correction request processing
- [ ] Manage cross-border transfer contracts and safeguards
- [ ] Conduct regular staff training and internal audits
- [ ] Serve as point of contact for PDPC inquiries

## Documentation
- [ ] Maintain DPO appointment letter/agreement
- [ ] Keep evidence of DPO independence and reporting line to senior management
- [ ] Update org chart and privacy policy with DPO info

**From compliance.py**: dpo_required=True, access_request_days=30, notes emphasize DPO designation and 30-day response.
